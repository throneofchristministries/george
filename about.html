About Us

Throne of Christ Ministries is a Bible-believing ministry committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, raising disciples, and transforming lives through prayer, teaching, and outreach. Our goal is to bring people closer to Christ and serve as a beacon of hope and faith in our community.

Our Vision

To reveal Christ to the world and raise spiritually grounded believers.

Our Mission

Winning souls, teaching the Word, and impacting generations through love, prayer, and active service.
